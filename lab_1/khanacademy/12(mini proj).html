Recipe #1 Omelet

  • Time: 10-15 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 2
Milk 50 ml
Salt 2 pinches

Step 1: Beat eggs with milk and salt.

Step 2:Heat oil or butter in a pan.

Step 2:Pour mixture and cook until ready(probably 10 minutes).

Recipe #2 Pasta

  • Time: 15-20 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 100g
Sauce 50g
Salt 3 pinches
Cheese 100g

Step 1: Boil pasta in salted water.

Step 2: Drain and add sauce.

Step 3: Top with cheese and serve.

Recipe #3 Sandwich

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 slices
Sausage 2 slices
Tomatoes 2 slices
Cheese 2 slices

Step 1: Put all ingredients on bread.

Step 2: Close sandwich.

Step 3: Toast.
